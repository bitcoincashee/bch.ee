5. Payouts

Payouts are issued when the pool finds a block. The payout structure is as follows:

  • The miner who finds the block receives a 1 BCH finder bonus, paid after 100 confirmations (~16.5 hours).
  • 99% of the remaining block reward (Block Reward + Transaction Fees − 1 BCH) is distributed proportionally among all miners based on shares submitted since the last block.
  • 1% is retained as the pool fee.

Payouts are sent automatically to the BCH address used as your mining username. No manual withdrawal is required. The pool does not hold funds beyond what is necessary to process the current block payout.

6. Fees

The effective fee is approximately ~0.7% of the full block reward. The 1 BCH finder bonus is paid fee-free; the 1% fee applies only to the remaining distributable reward. It is allocated as follows:

  • bch.ee Parasite Pool operator — 0.98%
  • asicseer-pool developer (Calin) — 0.01%
  • BCHN donation — 0.01%
